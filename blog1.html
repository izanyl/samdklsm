Development of The Lion Logo

Each animal seems to have a respresentation of some kind. To me the lion represents strength. I have always been interested in tigers, lion, or just about any wildcat. I noticed many sports team have some sort of animal as their mascot, but I have rarely seen lions used on sport teams. Although, I do see lion logos in businesses and cooperations. I have created many logos over the years, but I was always indecisive when it came to choosing a logo for myself. I have grown as a person over the past years, despite the fact that there were many hardships to deal with. As a lion represents strength, I feel as if I too have a lot of strength, mentality wise. That is why I chose the Lion as my main logo. The mark under the eye of the lion is supposed to represent its mysterious and menacing portion. The colors are unique to me because I have always liked the color Blue. There is however, secondary logos that I have also tied into my identity, and that is the Owl; an owl that represents mystery.
